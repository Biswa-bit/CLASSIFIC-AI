# Machine Learning & AI Research Papers

---

# Overview

Research papers are the foundation of modern machine learning and artificial intelligence.

Many of the algorithms, methodologies, and best practices implemented within CLASSIFIC-AI originate from academic research and industry publications.

This document provides a curated collection of influential research papers organized according to the CLASSIFIC-AI architecture.

The objective is to help contributors understand the theoretical foundations behind each agent while providing the RAG Agent with authoritative references.

---

# Purpose

The objectives are to:

- Build strong theoretical knowledge
- Connect implementation with research
- Support Explainable AI
- Encourage evidence-based development
- Provide learning resources for contributors
