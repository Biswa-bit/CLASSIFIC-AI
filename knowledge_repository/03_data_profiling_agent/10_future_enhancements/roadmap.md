# Development Roadmap

---

# Overview

This roadmap outlines the planned evolution of the CLASSIFIC-AI Data Profiling Agent.

The roadmap provides a long-term vision for enhancing automation, explainability, enterprise governance, artificial intelligence integration, and multi-agent collaboration.

It serves as a guide for contributors, maintainers, and users to understand the future direction of the Data Profiling Agent.

---

# Vision Statement

The Data Profiling Agent is the first analytical intelligence layer of the CLASSIFIC-AI ecosystem. Under the coordination of the Master Orchestrator Agent, it collaborates with specialized AI agents, contributes to the Enterprise Knowledge Repository, supports Retrieval-Augmented Generation (RAG), and powers the AI Copilot with explainable, context-aware insights. Together, these components form a unified enterprise platform for intelligent, trustworthy, and governed machine learning.
